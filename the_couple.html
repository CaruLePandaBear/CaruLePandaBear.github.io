Depending on who you ask, you may get two different stories about how we first met on that fateful night at the tail end of Hurricane Sandy. The “true story” (according to Jonathan) is that Hanna saw him from a distance and fell instantly in love. When Jonathan approached Hanna later in the night, he needed to say but a few words “I don’t believe we’ve met yet…” The rest is history. The other “true story” is that Jonathan spotted Hanna and asked her out. … womp womp.

But we agree on one thing, it was meant to be! From exploring the West Village, enjoying Riitta’s gourmet brunches in Amagansett, hiking the Santa Monica mountains in LA, to chillin with our main dog Panda Bear (AKA Karu) at our apartment in Flatiron, it's #hannathan forever!
